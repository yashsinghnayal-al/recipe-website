Pasta

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Prepare sauce with garlic and tomato.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce.
  4. Serve with cheese on top.
