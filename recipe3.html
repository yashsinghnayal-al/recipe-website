Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients well.
  2. Pour into baking pan.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  4. Cool before serving.
