Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Add salt and lemon juice.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Serve fresh.
